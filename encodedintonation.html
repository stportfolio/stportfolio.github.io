Encoded // Intonation

Continuing to work with sound recordings processed in to imagery I saw the opportunity for preservation of the ephemeral. Using recordings of people I frequently share meals with, I extracted short sound bites. Layering the encoded intimacy of sharing a meal, sharing a thought, and the surfaces that catalyze these moments I began producing tableware.

The sound bites were processed in to imagery, screen printed using colored slip on to plaster to be used for casting a porcelain slab picking up the image. These slabs were then draped over a positive mould of the plates shown here. Cups were also produced using a three part mould with fragmented bits of the imagery inlayed.
