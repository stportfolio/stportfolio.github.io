Laser Cut Textiles

Using meshes and lightweight synthetic materials to make new meshes allows me to dematerialize the material present, focusing on color and the spaces between the colors. The orderly use of pattern in a grid creates new meshes out of material. This causes liminal moments of clarity and distortion in the patterns as the viewer moves and interacts with the pieces.

Laser cut tulle
Installation dimensions variable, each panel 24" x 48"
