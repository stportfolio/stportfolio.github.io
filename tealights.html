Tea Lights

The double slit experiment shows that light can display characteristics of both waves and particles. Light, best demonstrated with a laser beam, passing through two closely spaced slits in a surface generates interference patterns. This duality was the inspiration the Tea Lights for the design. A singular light source, projects interference, perceived interference in harmony.

Stainless Steel, Cast Acrylic
5” x 3/4” x 3/4” to 5” x 4” x 4 1/2”
