Growth // Decay

Does our data need to travel with us?

A series of personal data storage devices. The GROWTH//DECAY USB jump drives began as a redesign of a "mundane object". These jump drives ask the user to examine the transportability of their data through the fragility of the jump drive its self or the obtrusiveness of the form.
